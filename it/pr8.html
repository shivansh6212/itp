B.Sc. Hons CS Course

Course Details

Fee: Please contact the university for fee details.

Eligibility Criteria: 10+2 with a minimum of 50% marks in PCM.

Papers and Credits

  • Introduction to Computer Science (4 credits)
  • Data Structures and Algorithms (5 credits)
  • Database Management Systems (4 credits)
  • Computer Networks (4 credits)
  • Operating Systems (4 credits)
  • Software Engineering (4 credits)

Future Possibilities

After completing the B.Sc. Hons CS course, students can pursue:

  • M.Sc. in Computer Science
  • Ph.D. in Computer Science
  • Software Development
  • Network Administration
  • Data Science and Analytics
